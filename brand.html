Color Palettes

#a00003

Steinhafel Red

Primary branding color. Used as base marketing color and for branded website features.

#800002

Structural Red

Primary accent color for structural website elements, such as links and icons.

#ba3c43

Highlight Red

Primary color used for link and button hovers.

#161616

Content Grey

Used for most textual content, including body copy and form labels.

#545454

Dark Grey

Used for secondary textual content such as supplementary text accompanying a heading.

#b0b0b0

Medium Grey

Division lines that separate blocks of content use this color.

#dadada

Light Grey

Structural accent color.

#e5e5e5

Accent Grey

Structural accent color.

#f0f0f0

Accent Light

Structural accent color.

#ffaa29

Accent Orange

Used for icons or elements that should stand out a bit more visually. Review star icons for example. Also for marketing text.

#fff387

Accent Yellow

Used as an accent for helper or informative content. Also for marketing text.
